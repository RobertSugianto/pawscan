PawScan

PawScan is a web-based app that uses AI to identify dog species from photos. Simply upload a picture, and the app analyzes its features. With a user-friendly design and extensive database, PawScan is perfect for pet lovers and playing outdoor enthusiasts!

How to Use

1
Take a Photo:
Use the camera feature to take a clear photo of the dog, or select an existing image from your gallery.
2
Upload the Photo:
Tap the upload button to submit the photo for analysis.
3
AI Analysis:
Wait a moment as the app's AI analyzes the dog's features.
4
View Results:
Review the identified species and read additional information about the dog
5
Save or Share:
Save the identification or share it with friends and family.

By following these steps, users can quickly and easily identify dog and enhance their knowledge of these dogs!
